Effects Of Polution On Animals

What kind of effects does pollution have on animals?

Many humans are unaware of the different effects pollution has on animals. Some external and habitual effects of pollution are environmental destruction, suffocation due to being tangled in plastics, and unsafe water conditions. Internal effects for animals would be the distruction of the digestive system from either an animal eating the plastic or eating an plant or smaller animal that has been exposed to acid rain or other toxins.

Why should we help?

In order to maintain a healthy ecosystem each plant and animal must be present to help the world funtion smoothly. Humans need a healthy ecosystem not only for recreational purposes, but also for medical, and agricultural for food and survival.
